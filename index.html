Intro:

Welcome to Tim Blommaert's unofficial Website.
Tim is a 27 yrs old Engineer from Ghent, who loves to sing and play music. In the past Tim has competed in Radio-contests organised by Studio Brussel,
And currently he is competing in the Belgian version of "The Voice".
Tim loves to share his talent with the world trough performing covers of songs, and
by bringing us some beautiful original songs. When u will browse trough this site you will be surely be moved by his performances.
